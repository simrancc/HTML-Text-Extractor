Details
Erstellt: 10. Februar 2017

This privacy policy discloses the privacy practices for WGSoft APPs (ScanMaster, MotoScan). This privacy policy applies solely to information collected by this applications and the choices you can make about the way information is collected and used. This policy notifies you of the following:

  • What information is collected from you while using this app, how it is used, and with whom it may be shared.
  • The security procedures in place to protect the misuse of your information.
  • How updates to this privacy policy will be communicated to you.

Information Collection, Use, and Sharing

When you purchase the App, we may collect the following information to help improve our products and services:

  • Your email address
  • The order number
  • Purchasing date
  • Application version

We don‘t sold or share this informationen with 3rd parties. This information is only used for software licensing and support purposes, so we can check whether you have purchased a license.

For debugging and troubleshooting purposes, we may ask you to send a debug log from the application. The information in the debug log is solely used to help address the problems that you are having with the app. If you choose to send the debug log, the following information is collected:

  • Application name and version
  • OBD2 communications, which includes information about your scan tool and vehicle
  • Your email address
  • Any error messages that may have occurred while you were using the application

Anonymous Information Collection

We do not collect any anonymous information from you.

Security

We take precautions to protect your information. When sensitive information is submitted via the app, the information is protected both online and offline. Wherever we collect sensitive information, that information is encrypted and transmitted to us in a secure way. While we use encryption to protect sensitive information transmitted to us, we also protect your information offline. The computers/servers in which we store the collected information are kept in a secure environment.

Updates

Our Privacy Policy may change from time to time and all updates will be posted on this page.
